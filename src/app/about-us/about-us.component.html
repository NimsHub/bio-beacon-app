Bio-Beacon

We're a team of passionate professionals who have dedicated our lives to developing the most innovative body area sensor network on the market. With Bio Beacon, you'll have access to real-time, data-driven insights that will revolutionize the way you train and compete. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just starting out, Bio Beacon is here to help you achieve your goals and take your athletic performance to the next level.

Bio Beacon is the ultimate tool for athletes looking to optimize their performance and reduce their risk of injury. Our state-of-the-art wearable body area sensor network includes three devices that measure everything from body temperature and blood pressure to respiration rate and body movement. All this data is then analyzed and visualized in our web application and mobile app, giving athletes real-time insights into their workload and allowing them to adjust their training accordingly. With Bio Beacon, athletes can take control of their performance like never before.
